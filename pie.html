Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1½ cups water
  • 2 lemons
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 pie crust

Directions

  1. Preheat Oven
    Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Make Filling
    Mix sugar, flour, cornstarch and salt.
  3. Prepare Meringue
    Beat egg whites until stiff peaks form.
  4. Bake
    Bake until golden brown.
